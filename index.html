Walking Mausoleums

What is it? A large stone structure on legs that walks across the map. Inside — a pedestal to duplicate a boss Remembrance.

What for? Each Remembrance offers a choice: weapon or spell. Duplication lets you get both options from a single victory.

How to bring it to its knees:
1. Approach from below → attack the white stone growths (calcified spots on the legs) — with weapon, arrows or spell
2. When it falls — enter inside → interact with the pedestal → choose which Remembrance to duplicate
3. One time per game — then it stands back up and is no longer accessible. Choose wisely.

With a bell on its head — any Remembrance. Without — only the 8 main Shardbearer bosses.

Elden Ring Status builds Effects

Status Effects — How They Work

Buildup accumulates with each hit and fades between attacks. When threshold is reached — proc. Important: Madness and Death Blight barely work on major bosses.

🦸
Bleed (Blood Loss)
Top-1 PvE & PvP
Effect~15% of max HP instantly on proc (burst, not DoT).
Buildup~40-80 regular enemies, 80-150+ bosses.
SynergyFrost→Bleed: Frost reduces negation -20% → Bleed hits harder.
vs BossesWorks on most bosses. Rare immunity.
RemoveStanching Boluses. Craft: Hefty Beast Bone + Land Octopus Ovary.
❄
Frostbite
Debuff + burst
Effect~10% HP burst + -20% damage negation + reduces stamina recovery for ~20 sec.
Buildup~20-50 regular enemies, 50-100 bosses.
SynergyFrost→Bleed: proc Frost → Bleed hits the weakened target harder.
vs BossesWorks on most major bosses. -20% negation = all damage hits harder.
RemoveThawing Boluses.
☠
Poison
Weak DoT
Effect~4-5 damage/sec DoT for ~90 seconds.
Buildup~40-60.
vs BossesWeak against major bosses (high resistance). Effective against world enemies.
RemoveNeutralizing Boluses.
🍄
Scarlet Rot
Strong DoT
Effect~15-20 damage/sec DoT for ~60 seconds.
Buildup~50-80 regular enemies, 100-200 bosses.
vs BossesEffective on nearly all including resistant bosses. DoT ignores some defenses.
RemovePreserving Boluses.
🔥
Madness
Burst + FP drain
EffectBurst HP damage + FP drain (up to 200+ FP).
BuildupFrenzied Flame zones and some enemies.
vs Bosses⚠ Only Tarnished and humanoid enemies! Most major bosses are fully immune. Effective: PvP, soldiers, NPC invaders.
RemoveClarifying Boluses.
💀
Death Blight
Near-instant death
EffectNear-instant death on proc.
Buildup~30. Rare.
vs Bosses⚠ Practically only Tarnished! Major bosses — almost always fully immune. Only effective in PvP or against weak NPCs.
RemoveNo standard Bolus. Avoid letting it accumulate.

Damage Combos — Best Status Chains

Different status types stack independently. Frost + Bleed = both active simultaneously. Two Bleed sources = one shared buildup pool.

Frost → Bleed → Frost → Bleed...
Most popular infinite cycle
Frost — 10% HP + -20% negation→Bleed — 15% HP burst→Frost again (-20% negation)→Bleed — 15% HP burst
Frost reduces damage negation by -20% → Bleed hits harder → after proc Frost buildup resets → repeat. Needs two weapons: Cold in left, Bleed in right. Endless cycle.
Build: Cold Uchigatana (L) + Rivers of Blood (R) | ARC 18+, DEX 18+
Frost → Fire → Frost...
Frost debuff + Fire burst
Frost — 10% HP + -20% negation→Fire damage→Frost again (-20% negation)
Frost reduces ALL negation types by -20% including fire → Fire attacks hit harder. Then Frost resets and you repeat.
Build: Cold weapon + Flame Grant Me Strength + Bloodflame Blade or Flame of the Fell God
Scarlet Rot + Poison simultaneously
Two DoTs stack independently
Rot — ~18 dmg/sec→Poison proc→DoT x2: ~25 dmg/sec
Rot and Poison are different status effects and run simultaneously. ~20 dmg/sec (Rot) + ~5 dmg/sec (Poison) = ~25/sec passive. Great against high HP tanky enemies.
Build: Antspur Rapier (innate Rot+Poison) | Or Rot incantation + Poison Grease
Frost → Rot → Bleed → Frost...
Three effects simultaneously — maximum pressure
Frost→Rot — ~18 dmg/sec→Bleed — 15% HP burst→Frost again (-20% negation)
The most complex but most powerful cycle. Frost debuff → Rot DoT active → Bleed burst → Frost again. Three statuses hitting simultaneously. Boss is constantly affected by effects.
Build: Rotten Breath (FTH 15) + Cold weapon + Rivers of Blood | Requires ARC 18+ and FTH 15+
Pre-buff → Frost proc → Bleed burst
Maximum damage in one window
FGMS + Flask→Frost — -20% negation→Bleed burst
Activate buffs first (FGMS + Flask Thorny+Stonebarb) → proc Frost (debuff -20% negation) → apply Bleed. Bleed burst at -20% negation + power buffs = maximum possible damage in one window.
Build: Quality or ARC/DEX | Requires prep before the fight
Frost → Holy / Lightning (specific bosses)
Against Malenia and Sacred enemies
Frost — -20% negation→Lightning or Holy
Malenia is weak to Fire and Lightning. After Frost proc (-20% to ALL negations) Lightning or Sacred hits much harder. Against Elden Beast: Holy is effective. Against Radagon: Holy is NOT effective.
Build: DEX/FTH hybrid | Lightning Armament + Cold Uchigatana

Never Do This

Boiled Prawn overwrites Flame Grant Me Strength
Prawn = Body buff. Always cast FGMS after Prawn.
Exalted Flesh overwrites Flame Grant Me Strength
Both are Body buffs. Last cast wins.
Bloodboil Aromatic overwrites FGMS and Exalted Flesh
Three Body buffs — mutually exclusive.
Howl of Shabriri overwrites Flame Grant Me Strength
If you want FGMS — cast it AFTER Howl.
Lightning Armament overwrites Bloodflame Blade
Two Armament buffs — second one wins.
Boiled Crab overwrites Exalted Flesh / FGMS
Crab = Body buff. Choose: damage or negation.
Rennala's Full Moon overwrites Ranni's Dark Moon
Similar debuff effect on enemies.

Casting Order

Correct Casting Order
1. Longest buff first: Golden Vow (80s) -> Crab (60s) -> FGMS (30s)
2. FGMS always last among Body buffs
3. Armament buff in any order — independent slot
4. Old Lord's Talisman extends all incantation buffs +30%

Great Runes — activated by Rune Arc

Active until death. First activate in the Divine Tower, then enable in Great Runes menu, then use a Rune Arc.

🌟Godrick's Great RuneS
+5 to ALL attributes
Divine Tower: Limgrave (via Stormveil Bridge)
Most versatile. Unlocks weapon requirements. Best early/mid. Less valuable after soft caps.
⚡Radahn's Great RuneS
+15% max HP, +15% FP, +15% Stamina
Divine Tower: Caelid (Redmane Castle — eastern bridge)
Top tier. Percentage bonuses more valuable after soft caps. +HP+FP+Stamina simultaneously.
🖤Morgott's Great RuneA
+25% max HP
Divine Tower: East Altus — Forbidden Lands lift
Simple and very effective. +25% HP = ~300+ HP endgame. Combo with HP regen.
🔥Rykard's Great RuneB
HP restore on kill: 80 + 7% max HP
Divine Tower: West Altus — via Sealed Tunnel
Useful in dungeons. Useless against solo bosses. Combo with Taker's Cameo talisman.
🩸Malenia's Great RuneB
Lifesteal ~5 sec after taking a hit; Flasks -30%
Divine Tower: Isolated Divine Tower (fast travel)
Complex mechanic. Flasks -30% — serious downside. Only for aggressive playstyle.
🩸Mohg's Great RuneB
Summons restore HP on kills
Divine Tower: East Altus (same as Morgott)
For co-op and Spirit Ash. Mimic Tear + Mohg rune = continuous HP regen.
